POS Fixed Amount Discount

Offer Fixed Amount Discount Method to Customers

POS Fixed Amount Discount Odoo app makes it easy for Odoo store admins to overcome the limitation of Odoo's default discount method, which is based on percentage. What our app does is gives Odoo store admins another option to keep a fixed amount discount option along with percentage. This is particularly helpful when the discount being offered cannot be converted in percentage.


The discount type displayed to the customers will be the discount type set by the admin from the backend. Also the customers will view the discount as per the discount type in different reports and documents like Invoice, Order Form View, Receipt, during Payment Order view, Invoice Report, Invoice form view and Sales Details Report.


Product Features

Set Discount in form of Percentage and Fixed Amount

Added Discount Type in POS Order form view

For Order Receipt Discount Details will be displayed as per the discount type

Added Discount Type in Invoice & Invoice Reports

The discount type will also be displayed in the previous invoices

Added Discount Type along with Discount in Sales Details Report

POS Fixed Amount Discount can also be implemented in Restaurant Management
